**Data Engineer**
Build system to collect and process data. Create foundation for all data work. Expertise centers on building reliable pipelines.

- Designing data infrastructure & architecture
- Creating and maintaining pipelines
- Ensuring data quality and availability

Data engineers deliver value through technical solutions. They connect different data sources seamlessly. They automate data collection processes. Their work enables all downstream data activities.

**Analytics Engineer**
- Exploration - explore data already ingested into data platforms in response to stakeholder questions and need
- Preparation - Cleaning and preparing datasets for analytics use cases
- Transformation - Transforming prepared datasets into objects that can serve objectives
- Documentation - Documenting the objects they find and create in the data warehouse

Analytics Engineers also allow for faster feedback loops since analysts with stakeholder context can work directly on maintaining and changing data pipelines.

Someone focused on developing and documenting data objects sets up analytics teams for data self-service with an accessible and understandable catalog of data products.

**Data Analyst**
Answer questions using data. Focused on solving business problems.
- Interpret data to find trends
- Creating reports and visualisations
- Working closely with business stakeholders

Data analysts provide value through actionable insights. They help businesses make informed decisions. They translate complex findings into clear recommendations. Their analyses drive strategic business outcomes
