### Sorting headers in tables and matrices

If categories do not sort how they should. Add a sort order column to the data, use a simple if in DAX or Power Query, or at source. Then in Power BI, select the categorical field, and on the top ribbon under *column tools* select *sort by column* and then choose the sort column. Now this data will defer to the sort order when arranged in tables or matrices etc.
